Half Life Alyx VR Interaction Research Proposal

Half-Life: Alyx is fantastic, but not many reviewers have talked in-depth about the design decisions around the VR controls of the game. In this brief video essay, I propose why I'm going to dive into the VR interaction design of Alyx to help other developers in VR and beyond.
